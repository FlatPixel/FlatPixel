Flat Pixel is a Paris based Game studio that is dedicated to creating visually stunning and innovative games using cutting-edge technology. With over 60 games and mini-games in Augmented Reality under our belt since 2017, we have developed a unique expertise in creating fun, engaging, and polished AR experiences.

Our team of talented artists and engineers, along with our international collaborators, work tirelessly to bring our clients' visions to life. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality games that pack a big punch and leave a lasting impression.

We believe in the power of collaboration and are always excited to work with clients who share our passion for creating unforgettable gaming and augmented reality experiences. So, if you're looking for a game studio that can bring your ideas to life with style and flair, don't hesitate to get in touch with us today!
